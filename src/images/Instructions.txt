# Instructions for background images and their buttons

### Background images
1. Pull background sources from data/gfx/ui/screens/
1. Ensure size is 1920x1080
1. Compress to ~78 quality (https://imagecompressor.com/ works)

### Buttons
1. Pull button sources from data/gfx/ui/events
1. Open in image editor (fireworks)
1. Crop edges and 1px of their inner shadows off
1. Resize to 100x100
1. Export with 80 jpg quality
